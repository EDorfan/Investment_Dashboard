How This Website Works

This investment dashboard is designed to help you track and manage your investment portfolio. Here's how it works:

  • Portfolio Tracking: Add your stock transactions and track your portfolio's performance over time.
  • Real-time Updates: Stock prices are updated daily at 6 PM to give you the most current portfolio value.
  • Visual Analytics: View your portfolio's performance through interactive charts and graphs.
  • Transaction History: Keep track of all your buy and sell transactions in one place.
